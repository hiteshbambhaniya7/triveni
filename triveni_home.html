Triveni Group, an Iron & Steel Industry enterprise, boasts nearly 70 years of experience as a family-owned business.

The group specialises in manufacturing of Hot Rolled and Cold Drawn Profile Sections, Bars, Squares and Rounds. These profiles include Critical shapes and sections, which adhere to international standards and also act as import substitutions for domestic markets.

The group also manufactures Precision Machined Components and Fabrication Parts with its in house Machining and Press Division facility. It provides One Stop Solution for its customers who wish to make their products as per their drawing and specifications.

Customer satisfaction is our motto and we ensure to supply superior quality material in timely manner to our customers.

Our Products

Cold Drawn Products – Bright Bars

Cold Drawn Products, commonly known as bright bars, are produced through a process called cold drawing. This technique enhances the surface finish, dimensional accuracy, and mechanical properties of the bars. They find application in various industries, including automotive, aerospace, construction, and engineering. Bright bars offer high strength, corrosion resistance, and excellent machinability.
